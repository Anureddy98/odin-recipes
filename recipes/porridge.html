"Porridge"

Description

Porridge is a wonderfully versatile and comforting meal that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or even dinner. Here's a basic recipe for classic creamy oatmeal porridge:

Ingredients

    cup rolled oats 2 Â½ cups water 1 cup milk of your choice (dairy or non-dairy) Pinch of salt, to taste

Preparation Method

    step-1:Combine the ingredients: In a saucepan, combine the rolled oats, water, and a pinch of salt. step-2:Bring to a boil: Place the saucepan over medium-high heat and bring the mixture to a boil. step-3:Simmer and cook: Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring frequently, until the liquid is absorbed and the oats are cooked through.This will likely take around 10-15 minutes. step-4:Add milk and simmer: Stir in the milk and continue to simmer for a few more minutes until the porridge reaches your desired consistency. If you like thicker porridge, you may need less milk, and if you prefer a thinner consistency, you can add a bit more.
